Event Specific Guidelines

1. Team Size: Each team must have 4 players (squad format).

2. Eligibility: Only registered students can participate, and college ID may be required.

3. Game Format: The tournament will have 1 to 2 round(mode: classic battle royal), and prizes are awarded based on placement.

4. Device: Players must use their own mobile devices, and emulators are not allowed.

5. Fair Play: Cheating, using third-party software, or teaming with other squads will lead to disqualification.

6. Match Conduct: Teams must check in on time, and if someone disconnects, the game will continue without a pause.
