Event Specific Guidelines

1. Pre-registration is mandatory; on-spot registrations will not be entertained under any circumstances

2. The total presentation duration is strictly 15 minutes, comprising 10 minutes for the presentation and 5 minutes for a Q&A session.

3. Each paper can have a maximum of 2 authors. Submissions exceeding this limit will not be considered.

4. A 200-300 word abstract is required for initial evaluation, outlining the research problem, approach, and anticipated results.

5. The top 10-15 abstracts will be shortlisted based on technical merit, innovation, and alignment with the presentation topics.

6. Shortlisted participants must submit the complete paper by the deadline. The paper may follow IEEE formatting standards for technical rigor and consistency.

7. Exactly 15 PowerPoint slides must be used for the presentation. Slides should be structured to convey key research insights, findings, and analysis concisely and effectively.

8. The top 3 papers will receive recognition and awards based on originality, depth of research, technical execution, and overall presentation quality.
