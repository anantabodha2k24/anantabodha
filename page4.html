Event Specific Guidelines

1. Originality: All submissions must be original, not copied from existing logos or designs. Plagiarism leads to disqualification.

2. Theme Alignment: The design should reflect the competition’s theme or the organization’s values and mission.

3. Submission Format: Logos must be submitted in specific formats (e.g., .JPEG, .PNG, or .AI), and at specified resolutions (e.g., 300 DPI).

4. Color and B&W Versions: Designers may be required to submit both color and black-and-white versions of their logo.

5. No Offensive Content: Designs must not contain offensive, inappropriate, or discriminatory elements.

6. Judging Criteria: Logos are judged based on creativity, relevance, simplicity, and scalability.

7. Deadline: All entries must be submitted by the specified deadline; late submissions will not be accepted.

8. Prize and Rights: The winner receives a prize, and the competition organizer typically acquires full rights to the winning logo.
