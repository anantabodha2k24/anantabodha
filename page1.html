Event Specific Guidelines

1. Online registration is mandatory for all participants.

2. Each team must consist of a minimum of two members.

3. The quiz will be conducted in three written rounds.

4. Participants will have 1 minute to answer each question. There will be a total of 45 questions, with each question carrying 1 mark.

5. Participants must bring their own pens and writing pads.

6. Team names must be clearly written at the top of each answer sheet.

7. The audience is strictly prohibited from providing any hints or assistance to the participants.

8. The use of mobile phones or any other smart devices is strictly forbidden during the quiz.

9. Answer sheets must be submitted on time. No corrections will be allowed after the final bell.

10. The top two teams will be selected as winners and will receive recognition and awards.
