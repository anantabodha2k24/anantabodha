SPHERICAL CLUSTER

Event Specific Guidelines

1. Build the tallest and most stable pyramid using 6 paper cups.

2. Form equal-sized teams.

3. Round 1: • Time limit for pyramid 1 construction is 10 minutes .

• The distance will be 15 meters.

• Pyramid must stand unsupported for 30 seconds.

4. Round 2: • Time limit for a second pyramid is 5 minutes.

• The distance will be 10 meters.

5. Construct within designated area.

6. If any cups fall during the construction process, players are required to retrieve them and return them to the designated starting point before continuing their pyramid assembly.

7. If any one of them in a team used their hands to construct the pyramid that team will be eliminated.

8. After both rounds, total scores will be calculated and to determine the overall winner.
