Event Specific Guidelines

1. Participation: As an individual, you will be the sole developer responsible for the entire project.

2. Time Limit: You will need to complete the project within the given time frame.( Eg: 1 hour)

3. Originality: All work must be your original creation.

4. Framework and Tools: You can use any web development languages, libraries, and frameworks (e.g., HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, etc.) Platform : Visual Studio Code.(This platform only allowed for webpage creation)

5.No Pre-Built Code: You are not allowed to submit pre-built or pre-designed websites. Any code you use from third-party sources must be properly attributed.

6. Topic : On spot topic .the webpage theme must be related to topic.

7. Design and Functionality: Your project will be judged on the overall design, creativity, usability, and functionality.

8. Submission: You must submit your project before the deadline. Late submissions may result in penalties or disqualification.

9. Live Demo: Be prepared to demo your website in real time, showcasing key functionality and features to the judges. Judging Criteria:

i. Creativity and originality.

ii. Relevance to the theme.

iii.Technical quality.

iv.Overall impact.

v.Time management.
