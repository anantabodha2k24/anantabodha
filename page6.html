SHUTTER SENSE

Capture the Moment in Shutter Sense!
Step into the world of Shutter Sense, where photography becomes an art of balance between objectivity and creativity. Photography is often seen as an impersonal medium, producing images untouched by personal feelings or biases. In this event, participants can explore both aspects: capturing unbiased, documentary-style photos or adding their own creative flair to make the images more subjective. Whether you prefer to point and shoot for raw, unfiltered images or manipulate your shots for artistic expression, this event challenges you to create compelling visuals that tell a story. Let your lens speak, and join the quest to find the perfect shot!

Event Specific Guidelines

1.File Format: JPEG or PNG are the most common formats.

2. File Size: There is usually a file size limit, such as 5-10MB per image.

3.Resolution: High-resolution images are preferred, often a minimum of 300 dpi.

4.Watermarks: No visible watermarks, borders, or signatures allowed.

5. Theme: Photos should adhere to the theme or category provided by the competition. Submissions outside the theme may be disqualified.

6. Originality: All submitted photos must be original and created by the contestant. No plagiarism or unauthorized use of another photographer's work.

7. Editing: Minimal editing like cropping, exposure correction, or color balancing is allowed. Overly edited or digitally manipulated images may be disqualified unless explicitly allowed.

8. Ownership: The image should not infringe on any third-party rights (e.g., trademarks, copyrighted materials).

Judging Criteria:

i. Creativity and originality.

ii. Relevance to the theme.

iii.Technical quality (focus, composition, lighting).

iv.Overall impact.
