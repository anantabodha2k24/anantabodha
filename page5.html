TREASURE HUNT

Embark on the Thrilling Treasure Hunt!

Join us for an exciting Treasure Hunt, where teams will solve riddles and tackle challenges to uncover hidden treasures. Navigate through a series of clues, explore different areas, and put your teamwork and problem-solving skills to the test. Race against other teams, crack the codes, and unlock the mystery. Prizes await the fastest hunters who complete the quest. Ready for an adventure? The treasure is waiting!

Event Specific Guidelines

1. Objective: Find all hidden clues that lead to the final treasure location.

2. Teams: Players can be divided into teams, each teams must have 5 players.

3. Starting Point: Each player/team starts at a designated location where they receive the first clue.

4. Clues: Players will follow a series of clues. Each clue leads to the next one.

Clues may involve solving puzzles, riddles, or finding hidden objects.

Maximum number of clues (10).

5. Time Limit: A time limit is 45 minutes for players to complete the treasure hunt.

The winner is the first to find the treasure or the team with the most clues found within the time limit.

6. Boundaries: Players must stay within designated boundaries during the game.

Wandering outside the set area results in disqualification.

7. No Cheating: Players must not sabotage other players or teams.

Sharing clues between teams is not allowed unless the game specifies it.

Mobile phones are not allowed.

8. Finding the Treasure: The team who finds the treasure first wins.

The treasure can be an object, a prize, or even a final task to complete.
