Event Specific Guidelines

1. Online Registration is mandatory.

2. Participants will be provided with a set of programs containing bugs, which must be identified and fixed within the time limit.

3. The competition will cover a predefined set of programming languages C, C++, Java, and Python.

4. The total number of questions for each participant is 5.

5. The competition will have a fixed time duration, typically ranging from 1 hour.

6. Points will be awarded based on the number of bugs identified and successfully fixed, with bonus points for efficient solutions.

7. Participants must submit their debugged code before the deadline; late submissions will not be considered.

8. Sharing or copying code from others is strictly prohibited and will lead to disqualification.

9. Any attempt to manipulate the system, cheat, or tamper with the competition platform will result in disqualification.

10. The decision of the judges is final, and any disputes must be raised immediately after the competition
